## For Volunteers

### How to Sign Up

1. Go to https://talktime.adeafoundation.org/
2. Click **"Sign Up"** or **"Get Started"**
3. Fill in your details:
   - Your full name
   - Email address
   - Create a password
   - Your phone number (optional)
   - Select your timezone
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. Wait for admin approval (you'll receive an email when approved)

### How to Log In

1. Go to https://talktime.adeafoundation.org/
2. Click **"Login"**
3. Enter your email and password
4. Click **"Sign In"**

### What You Can Do

**View Your Dashboard**
- See your upcoming meetings
- Check your meeting history
- View notifications

**Schedule a Meeting with a Student**
1. Go to your dashboard
2. Click **"Students"** in the menu
3. Browse available students
4. Click on a student's profile
5. Click **"Schedule Meeting"**
6. Pick a date and time that works for you
7. Click **"Confirm"**

**Join a Video Call**
1. When it's time for your meeting, go to your dashboard
2. Find the meeting in "Upcoming Meetings"
3. Click the **"Join Call"** button
4. Allow your browser to use your camera and microphone
5. Wait for the student to join
6. The call lasts 40 minutes

**Reschedule a Meeting**
1. Find the meeting on your dashboard
2. Click **"Reschedule"**
3. Pick a new date and time
4. Click **"Confirm"**

**Cancel a Meeting**
1. Find the meeting on your dashboard
2. Click **"Cancel"**
3. Confirm the cancellation

## For Students

### How to Log In

Students receive their login details from the school administrator.

1. Go to https://talktime.adeafoundation.org/student/
2. Enter your username (usually your admission number)
3. Enter your password
4. Click **"Sign In"**

### What You Can Do

**View Your Dashboard**
- See your upcoming meetings with volunteers
- Check past meetings

**Join a Video Call**
1. When it's time for your meeting, go to your dashboard
2. Find the meeting in "Upcoming Meetings"
3. Click the **"Join Call"** button
4. Allow your browser to use your camera and microphone
5. Wait for your volunteer mentor to join
6. The call lasts 40 minutes

**View Meeting Details**
- Click on any meeting to see:
  - The volunteer's name
  - Date and time
  - Meeting status

## Helpful Tips

### Before Your Video Call

1. **Check your internet connection** - Make sure you have stable internet
2. **Find a quiet place** - Choose somewhere without background noise
3. **Test your camera and microphone** - Make sure they work before the call
4. **Be on time** - Join the call a few minutes early

### During the Call

- Speak clearly and slowly
- Look at the camera when talking
- If you can't hear the other person, check your volume
- If the video freezes, wait a moment - it usually fixes itself

### If Something Goes Wrong

- **Can't log in?** - Check that you typed your email and password correctly
- **Video not working?** - Refresh the page and try again
- **Call disconnected?** - Click "Join Call" again to reconnect
- **Need help?** - Contact support at support@adeafoundation.org

## Important Rules

### For Volunteers

- You can have **1 meeting per day** with each student
- You can have up to **3 active meetings** scheduled with the same student
- Each call lasts **40 minutes**
- If you miss too many meetings, your account may be restricted

### For Students

- You can have **1 meeting per day**
- Always join your scheduled meetings on time
- If you can't make a meeting, let your volunteer know
