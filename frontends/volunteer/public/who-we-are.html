Who We Are

ADEA Foundation is bridging cultures and transforming lives by connecting Maasai youth in Kenya with compassionate volunteers worldwide through the power of conversation.

Learn Our Story Get Involved

Our Mission & Vision

Building bridges of understanding and opportunity across continents

Our Mission

To empower Maasai youth through accessible English language education, fostering cross-cultural understanding while preserving indigenous heritage and creating pathways to economic opportunity.

Our Vision

A world where every young person, regardless of their background or location, has access to quality education and the opportunity to connect with global communities while celebrating their unique cultural identity.

About ADEA Foundation

The ADEA Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to advancing educational opportunities for underserved communities in Africa. Our flagship program, TalkTime, represents our commitment to innovative, culturally-sensitive educational solutions.

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do at ADEA Foundation

Cultural Respect

We celebrate and preserve the rich Maasai heritage while fostering global connections.

Equity & Access

Education should be accessible to all, regardless of geographical or economic barriers.

Innovation

We embrace creative solutions that leverage technology to break down barriers.

Community

Building strong, supportive networks that empower both students and volunteers.

Sustainability

Creating lasting change through programs that can grow and adapt over time.

Compassion

Every interaction is guided by empathy, patience, and genuine care for others.
