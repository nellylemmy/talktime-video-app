TalkTime

TalkTime connects global volunteers with Maasai students in Rombo, Kenya, for free English practice chats. Powered by the ADEA Foundation, it builds confidence and skills through one-on-one conversations, opening doors to brighter futures. We have two types of volunteers

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between volunteers and Maasai students, fostering connections that build English fluency and open doors to new opportunities.

Focused Conversations

Enjoy simple, one-on-one 40-minute video calls. Our platform pairs one volunteer with one student for a personal and impactful conversation.

Two Ways to Help

Sign up as a General Volunteer or as a Student Volunteer if you're studying abroad. Both roles offer the same rewarding experience.

Simple & Free

Our platform makes scheduling and joining calls a breeze. TalkTime is completely free for both volunteers and students, forever.

Be a Mentor

Be patient, encouraging, and ask open-ended questions. Your positive support is key to helping students feel valued and confident.

Powered by ADEA

TalkTime is a proud initiative of the ADEA Foundation, supporting the KKIS Program to keep students in school.
